The Bees Knees

The Bees Knees is a pop rock band originating from Glasgow, Scotland. The band consists of lead vocalist Bryan Teddy, guitarist Mack Tolkens, guitarist Andrew Green, bassist Kent Zulkee and drummer Freddie Fischer. They met at a pub in 2008 and began performing at local venues while balancing their music careers with day jobs.

Since becoming a full-time band in 2019, The Bees Knees have gained international recognition. Their unique sound blends catchy pop melodies with rock energy, creating performances that leave audiences wanting more.

Global Impact

The band's breakthrough came with their 2019 single "Glass Onion," which topped charts in 19 countries and earned them their first platinum record. Since then, they've released three studio albums, each showcasing their evolution as artists.

Known for their energetic live performances, The Bees Knees have headlined major festivals including Coachella, Glastonbury, and Lollapalooza. Their commitment to connecting with fans has built a loyal global following.
